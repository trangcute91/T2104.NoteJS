Overview of bread and cakes

It's important when you start learning how to bake that you need to know the basics of how to distinguish between different types of bread and cakes, basic baking methods, fermentation methods, and how to identify the different types of cake. equipment in the bakery.

In the world of colorful cakes and countless cakes and breads, you must have been mesmerized by the irresistible attractive appearance and delicious taste of cakes. However, those who are just starting to learn how to make cakes often face a lot of difficulties because not everyone has the basic knowledge to start learning the simplest and easiest cakes.

So in the first article of the section on the meaning of popular cake lessons, we guide you through the most basic steps to start on the path of learning to bake a popular cake!

Distinguishing Types of Bread and Pastry

Bread: are cakes formed from basic ingredients such as flour, yeast, salt, water and other additional ingredients to create a porous, chewy, crispy and characteristic cake. by type. There are two main types of bread: flat bread and sweet bread.

Surely, all of us have enjoyed loaves of burgers, rolls, and cotton balls with a crispy crust on the outside and soft and chewy inside. It is one of the types of flat bread, the characteristics of flat bread are flat taste, using little or no sugar and fat in the recipe. Meanwhile, sweet breads are cakes with soft shells, spongy insides and are often combined with other ingredients to create shapes.

Cakes: is the common name of baked goods with a fluffy, fluffy, soft texture and ingredients with a lot of sweetness and fat. Cakes of various types are combined with a variety of decorative creams, sauces and jam fillings. In Vietnam, you are used to calling it sponge cake. There are also other lines such as: quick bread, Pie/tart and cold cakes like Custard – Mousse – cheese.

Why is it important to know the difference between breads and pastries? Each type of cake and bread has different ingredients, recipes and baking techniques. It is possible for a cake to use the same ingredients but different ingredients and mixing methods will create two completely different cakes. Therefore, distinguishing between types of bread and cakes is a must-have for anyone learning to bake.
