NEWS

Don't know what to eat in this weather?

Temporarily forget the hot, greasy dishes of the cold season, this summer we need to "condition the body" with cool, enchanting dishes. Just imagine in the hot weather in Hanoi, how happy and cool it is to be hiding in a familiar shop enjoying colorful boxes of ice cream and fragrant yogurt.

1.Tirano ice cream

Fresh ice cream, all colors, all flavors are what customers can notice right from the moment they hold a cup of Tirano ice cream of Anh Hoa Bakery. Tirano ice cream has been present at Anh Hoa Bakery system for many years. With many flavors to choose from: chocolate, taro, strawberry, vanilla, passion fruit… you will surely be immersed in the world of cool and sweet ice cream.

2.Naturally fermented yogurt

Anh Hoa Bakery yogurt is made from fresh milk and water and contains many nutrients such as potassium, calcium, magnesium, vitamin B2 and vitamin B12. Eating yogurt every day will help a lot for health, so this summer yogurt will be an effective food, right?

Not only delicious, cool, yogurt also has unmatched effects such as:
Soothes the digestive system, hypotension, prevent infection, improve immune system, enhance bone health, helps the body recover faster, helps to lose weight effectively

3.Caramen (Caramelisation)

People often know Caramel with a sweet, fragrant, greasy color. Eating Caramel this season is really cool and flavorful. On hot sunny days, Anh Hoa Bakery's Caramel poured into the store more and more. Do not hesitate to come to Anh Hoa Bakery to enjoy!

4.Mousse cake

Known as a cake with a lot of cream, a cake that is processed very sophisticatedly, thoughtfully and meticulously to keep the natural flavor of the fruit.

Mousse cakes are the perfect combination of greasy fresh cream, Cheese cream, egg yolk with delicious homemade fruit jam. Mousse cakes from Anh Hoa Bakery are the spirit children that are both sweet, frugal and soft and delicious.

The cake gives people a new feeling, sweet but also attractive. In this weather, don't miss the delicious fruit mousse cakes from Anh Hoa Bakery!
