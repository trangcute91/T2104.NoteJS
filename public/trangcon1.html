5 best side dishes when drinking coffee that you can't ignore

Nowadays in Vietnam, many coffee shops often sell cakes to accompany them to meet the needs of more diners according to modern trends. The cakes in Vietnamese cafes are often very rich. In which, there are certainly many cafes that sell 6 cakes that Westerners love to eat with coffee below.

1.Flan

The first cake I would like to introduce to you is Flan cake, a very popular cake today. Cake is a combination of sophistication and art of France.

The cake is made from the main ingredients of egg yolk, milk, sugar and caramel. The cake is easy to eat so it appeals to most different ages. Today, flan has become a popular cake in cafes and is loved by many people. The cake has a moderate sweetness, fragrant aroma, and helps reduce the bitterness of coffee but does not make us feel too sweet when drinking sugary coffee. Not only contributing to the deliciousness, but Flan cake is also an extremely nutritious cake suitable for young people, people who exercise and even those who want to lose weight. So when drinking coffee, you should definitely try it with Flan cake once.

2.Mousse Cake

Mousse Cakes originated from France. Mousse Cake is made from egg yolks, fresh cream, fresh milk, sugar and decorated with various eye-catching sources, most commonly strawberries. When eating the cake, the enjoyer will feel the surprise when there is a lot of cream inside, soft, cool, interwoven in many different positions and gradually melts in the mouth.

Mousse Cake is very loved by the French when drinking with coffee. In particular, the French often invite each other this cake to express their happiness to the other. This cake is perfect for a meaningful anniversary. The red of the strawberry, the cake has the sweet, sour, and bitter taste of cocoa – beautiful colors and flavors and perfect for a happy day. Currently, not only in France, many cafes around the world have brought this cake to their menus.

3.Hummus Wrap

The next dish that we mention on this list is the Hummus Wrap. Surely speaking here many of us have heard the name of this cake. Accordingly, Hummus Wraq is a popular dish in Middle Eastern countries.

It is made from pureed cooked chickpeas mixed with tahini (sesame butter), olive oil, salted lemon juice and garlic. Over time, this dish spread to many European countries and one thing that they love very much is to enjoy them while drinking coffee. This dish is not only delicious but also nutritious. Hummus is rich in iron and vitamin C. Chickpeas are a rich source of protein and dietary fiber, methionine. Hummus is useful for vegetarians and vegans. The Jews see this as their food. If possible, find and enjoy this delicious dish!

4.Cheese Cake

Perhaps Cheese Cake is the most familiar cake to many young Vietnamese among the cakes on this list. Now, let's take a closer look at this cake.

Ingredients to make this cake usually include biscuits, butter, sugar, eggs and cream Cheese. Accordingly, depending on the culinary characteristics of each country, there are many different types of Cheese Cake. In Europe, an indispensable ingredient in their Cheese Cake is cheese. The extremely attractive form, delicious and greasy taste of this cake has "fallen in love" with countless diners. And enjoy it in a romantic space with a cup of coffee.

5.Pana Cotta

If Cheese Cake is a familiar cake, Pana Cotta is a strange cake for many food lovers, especially fans of cakes.

Today's Panna Cotta is always made with gelatin in the ingredients to create a moderate consistency and smooth feel for the cake. With this special texture, the piece of cake when eaten will have a very interesting feeling of melting in the mouth. Pana Cotta cakes are made mainly from butter, milk, pureed fruits. Depending on the favorite fruit of each person, the taste of Pana Cotta cake is quite diverse. Try it with coffee to enjoy great moments of food and drink combinations.

6.Tiramisu

Tiramisu, perhaps when reading this name, many people think that it is of Japanese origin because the name sounds "very Japanese". But in fact, this is a cake originating from Italy. This cake is made from rum, coffee, cocoa, cream, cheese and eggs.

Many Western countries like to eat this cake when drinking coffee. Or to Italy, you can easily see couples drinking coffee and eating Tiramisu together in a very romantic space. Let's search and experience the taste when drinking coffee and enjoy this cake right now! Above are 6 cakes that Westerners like to eat with coffee the most. Hope the content that Vietnamese Legend has just shared gives you a lot of interesting information!
